# Billing Revenue Page Overrides

> PROJECT: Clinical Atelier
> Page Type: Finance operations dashboard

These rules override the master design system for the `/billing-revenue` route.

## Layout

- Keep the first viewport focused on KPI scanning: revenue today, month-to-date, pending invoices, pricing coverage.
- Use one dense ledger panel and one analytical panel side-by-side on desktop.

## Required Sections

1. Revenue summary metrics row.
2. Department revenue breakdown.
3. Invoice ledger with filters.
4. Pricing rules visibility panel.

## Tone

- Operational and precise, not marketing-heavy.
- Green means captured revenue; amber means pending finance attention.
