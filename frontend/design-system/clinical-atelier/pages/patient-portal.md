# Patient Portal Dashboard Overrides

> PROJECT: Clinical Atelier
> Page Type: Authenticated patient home

These rules override the master design system for the `/patient-portal` route.

## Layout

- Keep the first screen reassuring and simple.
- Lead with upcoming care and only then surface navigation to deeper records.

## Required Sections

1. Overview metrics.
2. Next appointment card.
3. Quick links to appointments, lab results, messages, and profile.

## Tone

- Patient-friendly and calm.
- Avoid internal operational language.
