# Public Home Page Overrides

> PROJECT: Clinical Atelier
> Page Type: Public marketing + trust-building

These rules override the master design system for the `/` route.

## Layout

- Use a full-width hero with centered content and a clear primary booking CTA.
- Keep the top navigation lightweight: home, departments, doctors, booking, news.
- Maintain strong vertical rhythm with distinct content bands rather than dense card walls.

## Required Sections

1. Hero with primary booking action and secondary doctor/departments action.
2. Trust metrics or proof strip near the fold.
3. Department exploration grid.
4. Featured doctors or specialists.
5. Booking flow explainer with 3-4 steps.
6. News or hospital updates.
7. Footer with contact and utility links.

## Tone

- More editorial and reassuring than dashboard-like.
- Use larger type, wider spacing, and fewer simultaneous actions per section.
- Prioritize trust, clarity, and accessibility over novelty.

## Components

- Use cards for departments and doctors, but avoid over-nesting.
- Keep CTAs explicit and high-contrast.
- Testimonials are optional; clinical trust metrics are preferred.

## Accessibility

- The hero CTA must be keyboard reachable without passing through the full nav.
- Keep all touch targets at or above 44px height.
- Preserve the skip link and visible focus rings.
