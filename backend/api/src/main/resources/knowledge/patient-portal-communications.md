# Patient Portal Communication SOP

## Lab result handling
- Reviewed lab results can be surfaced to staff and patient portal users together with a short clinical summary.
- Staff should rely on the reviewed summary and doctor comment rather than raw interpretation when answering portal questions.

## Message thread handling
- Patient message threads should be used for operational follow-up, clarifications, and preparation instructions.
- Staff responses must stay factual, avoid diagnosis beyond the saved medical record, and cite the latest approved note when possible.

## Privacy boundary
- Internal staff tools may summarize portal messages only within the permitted patient context.
- Administrative users without patient context must use document mode and cannot retrieve PHI through the assistant.
