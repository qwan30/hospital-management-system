# Medical Record Completion Policy

## Required documentation
- Doctors must complete diagnosis, clinical notes, and any vital sign adjustments before closing the encounter.
- The note must stay read-only after final confirmation except through the approved correction process.

## Prescription and PDF handling
- If medication is prescribed, the record must include medicine name, dosage, frequency, duration, and instructions.
- The prescription PDF is generated from the final medical record and is the approved artifact for patient delivery.

## Follow-up planning
- When a follow-up is required, the doctor must record the follow-up date and the reason for the revisit.
- Follow-up scheduling must stay linked to the medical record so reminder jobs can use the saved date safely.
